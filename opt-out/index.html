ScaleMonk Media Opt-Out Information

Who served this ad?

This ad was served by ScaleMonk: ScaleMonk is an advertising exchange that connects advertisers and mobile app publishers to serve ads to audiences that may be interested in their products or services.

Why am I seeing this ad?

To create a more customized online experience, ScaleMonk may collect information about your device and the ads you view or click. When you download, visit, or interact with the app or website of a ScaleMonk advertiser or publisher, ScaleMonk may collect information in connection with that activity.

Please read ScaleMonk's privacy policy to know more about what data ScaleMonk collects and how it is used.

What choices do I have?

ScaleMonk respects your privacy as a user, and you can opt-out from receiving interest-based advertising from ScaleMonk on supported devices:

  • • Your iOS mobile device may provide a "Limit Ad Tracking" setting. On Android devices, your settings may allow you to "Opt out of Interest-Based Ads." When you have opted out using this setting on a device, ScaleMonk will not use in-app information collected from that device to infer your interests or serve ads to that device that are targeted based on your inferred interests.
  • • Your mobile web browser may also provide a "Do Not Track" browser setting. When you have enabled this setting in your browser, ScaleMonk will not use mobile web browsing information from that browser to infer your interests or serve ads to that device that are targeting based on your inferred interests.

Please note that while opting out limits ScaleMonk's collection of information to serve you ads based on your inferred interests, it does not prevent you from receiving ads from ScaleMonk, Twitter, or third parties.


How Do I Find My Device ID?


Apple Devices

If you are using an Apple mobile device, the following iOS app will help locate your IDFA: The Identifiers.

Android Devices

If you are using an Android device, To locate Android ID, you must install the "Device ID" app from Google Play. To download, click Device ID.

Other Devices

We are not able to instruct you on how to find you Device ID if you are not using an Apple or Android platform. Please check back with us, as we are continually working on enhancing tools for other platforms.

If you want to completely opt-out receiving ads in your mobile device, please enter your Device ID and click 'Opt-Out'. Please allow 30 days for processing.

Questions?

If you have additional questions or would like further information on our Privacy Policy, please email us at contact@scalemonk.com.
